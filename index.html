Who are we?

We are a small flexible team of developers and artists with a passion to make games and software. Not only the final product is our focus, but we also highly value the way we get there. To accomplish this we work in short development cycles to ensure that the product is always moving in the right direction. At each step of the way we make sure that the right professional is on the job.
